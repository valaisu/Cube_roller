The cube game

The main things players do in this game is building cubes and rolling them. The game is played 1v1.

So we have a board, something like 5x5, I have not decided yet. The players take alternating turn.
During their turn, player can either
 - roll their cube
 - start building a new cube
 - add sides to a cube that is under construction

The objective, well, I have not quite decided what that is. 
Options / ideas
 - invade more space than your opponent
 - perform specific action at enemy "base"
 - reach enemy base 

So the cubes can be rolled, and they can be "builded" but what does that mean?
My idea was, that you can add functionalities to each side of the cube, and they trigger, when 
the side is facing up after moving the cube. Ideas for such mechanics:
 - Slide forward / sideways / any direction
 - Push: Slide adjacent friend / opponent one away straight / in any dir
 - Fortify: cannot be moved by other effects / destroyed
 - Grapple: bring cube next to this cube from specific / any direction
 - Detonate: destroy any adjacent cubes (4 / 8 tiles) 
 - Start: This does nothing, the cube has to start this side down (should this also have a stronger and weaker variant?)
 - Rotate: roll or rotate in place / do both
 - Power: If this face is facing up, the cube is allowed to move even if blocked in some direction. The cube that is blocking
   this cube will slide to the movement of the direction. If there is no empty space,the power cube cannot move / the blocking 
   cube is destroyed
 - Build: Add a new effect or upgrade to an existing cube in docs / anywhere 

Yeah so the idea is that for each effect, there is the weaker and the stronger variant. 
The stronger variant can be obtained by building first the weaker one and then building over that

notes about mechanics:
 - Slide: you can only slide to an un-occupied space
 - I have not yet decided if triggering the mechanics should be optional or mandatory. Maybe upgrading the starting side
   could make it optional?
